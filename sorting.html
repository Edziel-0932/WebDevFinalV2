Manual Sorting

Workers separate e-waste by hand using bins and containers for plastics, metals, and glass.

Mechanical Sorting

Uses conveyor belts, shredders, and magnets to sort materials efficiently.

Automated Sorting

Advanced systems use sensors, AI, and robotics to identify and separate components.
